Our mission

Our purpose is to create informed and excited investors who feel connected to Cadro’s incredible team and confident about reaching their financial goals.
We’re here to revolutionise your investment journey, so whether you’re talking with your Cadro client adviser or watching tailored market insights on the exclusive Cadro app, you can rest safe in the knowledge that we’re here for you, whenever you need.

The Cadro code

-A

All together: for you

Cadro provides you with access to experts across the entire breadth of our team. We’ve assembled individuals from diverse backgrounds and created an environment of innovation and inclusivity for your benefit.
-B

Better ‘til best

We have big ambitions – amazing products, awesome service – but we’re always looking for ways to improve.
-C

Communication

We communicate clearly and love to listen. We make things easy and enjoyable to understand. We can because we know what we’re talking about.
-D

Destination in sight

We take a long-term approach to investing, but we stay nimble and open to change. We act decisively, with clear aims in mind.
Cadro brings together experts with diverse skillsets, who seek to challenge the status quo.
Whether it’s our combined decades of experience investing in public and private markets, our world-beating tech engineers, or our one-of-a-kind content producers, we’re focused on making Cadro truly unique for investors, whatever their ambitions.
